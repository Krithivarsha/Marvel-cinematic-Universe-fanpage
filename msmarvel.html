MS MARVEL

"I always thought I wanted this kind of life, but I never imagined any of this."

Kamala Khan Kamala Khan is a Djinn-human hybrid and a student at Coles Academic High School who is a fan of the Avengers, particularly Captain Marvel. Possessing dormant genetic powers that were activated when she wore a bangle of unknown origin, she became a superhero in Jersey City dubbed Night Light by the public before being known as Ms. Marvel.

She decorated her room with several pictures and artwork of Danvers and made herself a Captain Marvel cosplay costume. In addition to that, she also drew a picture of Danvers' pet, Goose. With her vivid imagination and plenty of inspiration, she started a YouTube channel, where she talked about the Avengers. She listened to Scott Lang's podcast interviews and learned about the Battle of Earth and created drawings of the heroes of the battle. These drawings included Captain America, Iron Man, Thor, Hulk, Hawkeye, Wanda Maximoff, Ant-Man, Captain Marvel, Valkyrie, Black Panther and Okoye.

She then used those drawings in a video she created about the event, highlighting Captain Marvel's role in it, noting how she had destroyed the Sanctuary II. Before that video, she had made a video about Ant-Man and Wasp having a romantic getaway in Paris. As she began to sign off, she promised to make a two-part video series about Thor being a gamer. She then signed off by asking viewers to like the video, leave a comment, and subscribe to her channel.
